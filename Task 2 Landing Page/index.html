Our Courses

Computer Science and Engineering

Dive into the fascinating world of computer science and engineering. Explore cutting-edge technologies, develop software solutions, and master the art of programming. Our comprehensive curriculum covers topics such as algorithms, data structures, software engineering, computer networks, and emerging fields like artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Electronics and Communication Engineering (EEC)

Embark on a journey into the world of electronics and communication engineering. Learn to design and develop cutting-edge electronic systems, communication networks, and embedded systems. Our program covers topics like digital electronics, signal processing, telecommunication systems, and emerging technologies like 5G, IoT, and cybersecurity.

Mechanical Engineering

Unleash your creativity and problem-solving skills in the field of mechanical engineering. Design and develop innovative mechanical systems, products, and manufacturing processes. Our program covers topics such as thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, materials science, computer-aided design (CAD), and sustainable engineering practices.

About ADYPU

Ajeenkya DY Patil University (ADYPU) is India’s premier destination for higher education. Established under the Maharashtra Government Act of 2015, the university aims to shape an innovation-oriented Indian society. It is nestled in Pune, often called the ‘Oxford of the East’ for its educational eminence, within a vast 100-acre campus.
